Startup Help & Business Setup Guide

A complete checklist to launch and grow your business successfully.

1. Domain & Hosting

  • Domain Name – Short, memorable, brand-focused. Register with GoDaddy, Namecheap, or Google Domains.
  • Hosting – Cloud hosting (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) or premium shared hosting (SiteGround, Bluehost).
  • Ensure 99.9% uptime, SSD storage, and scalable resources.

2. Website Platform

  • CMS – WordPress (flexible, SEO-friendly) for most businesses.
  • E-commerce – Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento.
  • Custom Builds – React.js, Next.js, Laravel.

3. Design & UI/UX

  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
  • Fast loading (<3 seconds) with optimized images & caching.
  • Clear navigation & branding consistency.
  • Accessibility compliance (WCAG).

4. Core Pages

  • Homepage – Clear headline, value proposition, CTA.
  • About Us – Background, mission, team.
  • Services/Products – Details & pricing.
  • Contact Page – Form, phone, email, map.
  • Blog/News – SEO articles.
  • Privacy Policy & Terms – Legal compliance.

5. Technical Performance

  • SSL Certificate (HTTPS).
  • CDN (Cloudflare, AWS CloudFront).
  • Image optimization (WebP, lazy loading).
  • Browser/server caching.
  • Minified CSS/JS.

6. Security

  • Web Application Firewall (WAF).
  • Daily automated backups.
  • Anti-malware & anti-spam tools.
  • Secure admin with 2FA.
  • Regular updates to CMS, themes, and plugins.

7. SEO & Marketing

  • SEO-optimized structure (H1, meta tags, alt text).
  • Keyword research.
  • Schema markup.
  • Google Analytics & Search Console setup.
  • Social media integration.
  • Email subscription form.

8. E-commerce (if applicable)

  • Secure payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal).
  • Product catalog & filtering.
  • Cart & guest checkout.
  • Order tracking.
  • Automated invoices & notifications.

9. Maintenance & Scalability

  • Easy-to-update CMS.
  • Scalable hosting plan.
  • Staging environment for testing.
  • Performance monitoring (UptimeRobot, GTmetrix).
